Artificial Inteligence & Machine Learning





What is AI/ML?



Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are fields of computer science that involve the development of intelligent systems that can learn from and make decisions based on data. These fields are being applied in a wide range of industries, including healthcare, finance, and e-commerce.

Skils required

In AI/ML one should have a strong mathematics background and a knowledge of coding, who's core responsibilities are Designing and developing machine learning and deep learning systems, Running machine learning tests and experiments, Implementing appropriate ML algorithms.

we identified the following core skills one must possess for AI/ML

  • Programming skills needed
  • Computer science fundamentals and programming
  • Distributed computing
  • Machine learning algorithms and libraries
  • Software engineering and system design
  • Strong knowledge of data

    • Additionally, soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving are important for success in the field.

    Pros & Cons

    Pros of pursuing a career in AI and ML include the opportunity to work

    on cutting-edge technologies and make significant contributions
